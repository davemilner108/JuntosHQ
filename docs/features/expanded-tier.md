

# Feature: Expanded Tier ($9.99/month)

## What It Is

The Expanded tier provides generous scaling for dedicated users: coaches running multiple groups, community organizers, or anyone who wants substantial capacity without hitting walls often.

**Price**: $9.99/month (future: $99.99/year — 2 months free)

---

## What's Included

| Feature                      | Expanded Tier                     |
|------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Juntos                       | Up to 15                          |
| Members per junto            | 12                                |
| Log meetings                 | Yes                               |
| Visible meeting history      | Up to 80 per junto                |
| Commitment tracking          | Self-reporting enabled            |
| Weekly Franklin prompts      | Yes                               |
| Custom discussion prompts    | Yes                               |
| Member invite links          | Yes                               |
| Export (CSV/PDF)             | Yes                               |
| Priority support             | Yes                               |
| Ben Franklin Chatbot         | Available as add-on ($4.99/mo)    |

---

## Why $9.99 & These Limits

Double Standard price but still accessible. 15 juntos cover serious multi-group leaders (e.g., 10–12 active + a few backups). 80 meetings give ~1.5–2 years of weekly history — deep enough for long-running juntos without unlimited storage concerns.

Leaves room for future "Enterprise" tier if needed (e.g., 50+ juntos, team billing).
